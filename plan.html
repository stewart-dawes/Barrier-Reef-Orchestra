Mission Statement

The goal of this website is to increase the number of attendees at performances by 25%. The increase in audience members should mainly consists of younger attendees.

Success Evaluation

After the launch of the website we should see at least a 25% increase in the number of tickets sold to performances. The audience average age should also show a generally lower age, as younger people are expected to be drawn by the redesigned web site

Target Audience

The target audience for the website is men and women between the ages of 25-40 who are considering attending a performance as an audience member.
They may have some understanding of music, but not necessarily on a technical level.
The website will be characterised by a formal appearance, with the main colour theme being black and white (representing formality) and highlights of shades of blue to represent the barrier reef.
The formal look will appeal to the target audience as this age bracket is entering a mature stage in their life, while the shades of blue maintains the client's desire to display connection to the barrier reef.

An incidental audience for the web site is people considering joining the orchestra as either a playing or non-playing member.
This audience will be targeted through the presentation of in-depth information of the players and by being given the ability to submit a joining application online.

Content

The content of the web site will be structured so that a viewer may move to any first or second level page through the common format bar menu from any level page.
Second level pages will act as portals into third level page with only minimal information at this stage.
