Adam Lopez

Opera trained and two time Guinness World Record Holder for singing the highest vocal note by a male singer! (C Sharp in the 8th Octave - a semi-tone higher than a Grand Piano).

This achievement has astounded audiences from all around the world leading Lopez to perform and appear on international television and radio stations in Japan, Spain, Italy, Korea, Hong Kong and the USA.
