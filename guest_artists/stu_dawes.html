Stu Dawes

Sydney born, tubist, bassist and low brass specialist, Stu Dawes plays with community bands wherever his Army postings take him.

In 1997, he won 3rd place in the NSW band association open tubas and Euphoniums category. Since then there's been no looking back.

He is not very well known outside of the bands he has played in. Most recently, the Albury City Band in NSW and the Brisbane Symphonic Band in QLD.

Stu is known to be quite the prankster and smart alec- the main reason you're seeing his profile here. :)
