Mission Statement

The goal of this web site is two-fold: to increase membership to the orchestra (both playing and non-playing) and to increase audiences at performances. Audience increase should be characterised with younger attendees.

Success Evaluation

  1. Increased player and non-player membership
  2. Increased audiences at performances
  3. Increased number of younger audience members

Target Audience

  • The target audience for the web page is younger people who are considering attending a performance as an audience member.
  • An incidental audience for the web site is people considering joining the orchestra as either a playing or non-playing member.
